Who is Ellen?

I'm a front-end developer and recovering public relations professional.

I spent the first part of my career doing strategic communications and media relations in various industries—most recently I've launched startups and helped tech companies share their stories.

I taught myself how to code as a kid in 1997, making Geocities sites. I want to get back to that original passion and start actually building tech as a full-time web developer. I offer more than just technical ability to push pixels; my background in PR gives me a unique perspective on how code and content work together to effectively deliver brand messaging.

My personal interests include health and fitness—I run distances from the 5K to the marathon, stand-up paddleboard in the Hudson River, bike around Brooklyn and Manhattan, and I occasionally venture to Staten Island to golf.

I'm available for hire for web development projects, and select public relations work.
